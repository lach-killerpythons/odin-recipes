Flathead Fish & Chips - The best fish for fish & chips?

Anyone who lives in Sydney that has ever gone fishing knows that Flathead is the fish you want to catch

This is the last flathead I caught & how I cooked it!

Highly sort after for recreational fishing and fish & chips enthusiasts - there is not a better choice for Fish & chips

Sure there are cheaper more available options, but if you can catch a Flathead this is the best Fish & Chips you can have..

Ingredients

  • One flathead [Freshly Caught]
  • Plain flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Frozen chips
  • Tartar sauce
  • One Lime/Lemon

Instructions

  1. There is no easier fish to fillet than flathead - I will spare you the details but it's so easy
  2. Put those chips straight in the oven - make sure you follow the directions
  3. Lightly flour those fillets so they evenly coated
  4. Get that oil hot and listen to that sizzle when the fish hits the pan
  5. When that fish is looking golden brown on both side get ready to plate!
  6. Serve that flathead + chips with some limes & tartar sauce
